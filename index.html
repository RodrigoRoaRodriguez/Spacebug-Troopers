Awaiting command...
Catch those pesky little space bugs! Command your soldiers by saying their color and in what direction it should move.
Ex. "Blue up", "Red move to Orange"
You can also steer the soldiers using the arrow keys, and choosing soldier by pressing 1, 2 and 3.
Walk on the and say "activate", "freeze" or press E to make the monsters freeze for 2 steps. You can only do this once per game.
